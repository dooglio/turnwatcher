Ultra Initiative (Optional)

If you're one of those Game Masters that likes to mix up the action between rounds, select Ultra Initiative via the Rounds menu. Ultra Initiative mode causes Turn Watcher to automatically re-roll initiative at the top of each round.

Tip: Use Ctrl-U to turn on or off Ultra Initiative at any time while in or out of combat rounds!
